Glossary

NEMO IMS Glossary.

Key concepts for understanding Debunk’Onomy and the monetary doctrine of NEMO IMS.

NEMO IMS

NEgentropic MOney International Monetary System: a proposed international monetary system oriented toward ecosystemic robustness.

NEMO Green DTS

Debt-free international monetary units dedicated to regenerative, low-impact or robustness-building activities.

Monetary melting

The programmed destruction of money during transactions, weighted by ecological and social impact.

Fixed-exchange web

An exchange architecture in which currencies are linked to a neutral standard rather than to a dominant reserve currency.

Ecosystemic robustness

The capacity of societies and ecosystems to absorb shocks without collapsing into degradation.

Degenerative activity

An activity that increases ecological overshoot, social fragility or systemic dependence.

Regenerative activity

An activity that restores, protects or strengthens the conditions of life.
