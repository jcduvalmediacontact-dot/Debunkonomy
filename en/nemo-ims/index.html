NEMO IMS

A monetary architecture for planetary boundaries.

NEMO IMS is a proposed international monetary system designed to align money creation with ecological regeneration, social robustness and planetary limits.

Receive the Debunk’Onomy letter →Start hereDiscover NEMO IMS

NEMO IMS in five sentences

  1. The current system creates money mainly through debt.
  2. Debt imposes a permanent requirement for growth and profitability.
  3. This requirement collides with planetary boundaries.
  4. NEMO IMS creates debt-free money to finance regenerative activities.
  5. This money is progressively destroyed during transactions according to their ecological and social impact.

Three pillars

Debt-free regenerative money

NEMO Green DTS would finance activities that restore ecosystems, strengthen robustness or reduce systemic fragility.

Weighted monetary melting

Transactions would carry a monetary melting rate reflecting their ecological and social impact.

A fixed-exchange web

Currencies would be connected to a neutral exchange standard, limiting monetary hegemony and reserve-currency dependency.
